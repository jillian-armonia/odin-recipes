Fish with Tofu in Oyster Sauce

Description:

A healthy meal packed with lots of flavor. You can add more vegetables to this dish as you like!

How to Make Fish with Tofu in Oyster Sauce

Ingredients:

    Fish and Tofu:

  • 300g Fish Strips (about half an inch per strip)
  • 3 Tofu Cubes (cut into strips, same size as the Fish strips)
  • 1 cup of Seasoned Flour
  • 1 large egg
  • Canola Oil (for frying)

    • Sauce:

  • 1 medium sized Red Bellpepper
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 whole medium sized onion
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (or the amount you prefer)
  • 1 small pack oyster sauce (I used Mama Sitaâ€™s)
  • 1 small pack Black Beans
  • 1 tsp flour (to thicken the sauce)
  • 1 to 2 tbsp minced parsley (or the amount you prefer)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2tbsp of brown sugar(if you want to add sweetness)
  • a dash of Sesame oil

Instructions:

    Fish and Tofu:

  1. Coat Tofu and Fish strips with egg, then dip into the Seasoned flour for the final coating.
  2. Fry both Tofu and Fish until Golden Brown. Set aside.

    Sauce:

  1. Saute bellpepper,ginger, garlic, and onion. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and Black Beans. Let simmer for about a minute.
  2. Add flour. Season with salt, pepper, and brown sugar. Add the fried Tofu and Fish Strips. Mix well until everything is well combined.
  3. Add parsley and Sesame oil. Serves 4-6pax. Enjoy!
