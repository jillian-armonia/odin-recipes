Baked Salmon Recipe

Description:

Easy to make and delicious baked salmon that you can serve in house parties.

How to Make Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Real Mayo
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 tsp ground red pepper (cayenne)
  • 4 skinless salmon fillets (1 lb.)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 10 round buttery crackers, crushed (about 1/2 cup)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Mix mayo, cheese and pepper until blended.
  3. Place fish in shallow foil-lined pan; drizzle with lemon juice. Cover with mayo mixture; sprinkle with cracker crumbs.
  4. Bake 12 to 15 min. or until fish flakes easily with fork.
